Thesis & Key Metric

annual / thesis change

Falsifiable Thesis Statement

Robinhood's product velocity and vertical integration are enabling it to capture disproportionate share of the millennial/Gen-Z wealth accumulation cycle, sustaining 25%+ revenue CAGR through FY2028. Three pillars: (1) expanding ARPU from $171 toward $250+ through Gold, credit card, banking, retirement; (2) new revenue verticals — prediction markets, futures, international — adding $500M+ incremental by FY2027; (3) maintaining 45%+ operating margins as fixed-cost leverage offsets investment.

Falsification trigger: Net deposit growth below 15% YoY combined with funded customer churn exceeding 2M would challenge this thesis.
Date written: March 15, 2026  ·  Last reviewed: March 15, 2026

3-Sentence Bear Case

PFOF regulatory risk (Reg NMS: tick size + access fee reduction Nov 2026, Rule 605 disclosure Aug 2026) could compress margin below 40% — 59% of revenue has outsized PFOF exposure; a 20% decline cuts total revenue ~12%. Competition from Schwab (38.5M accounts), Fidelity (45M), and SoFi erodes differentiation as rate cuts ($304M/100bp) compress NII (34% of revenue) and crypto cyclicality drives volatility. Heavy executive selling ($1.4B+, zero purchases, co-founder Bhatt near-complete economic exit) signals potential misalignment with the public growth narrative.

Business Model & Product Architecture

annual / thesis change
1. Options & Equities Trading
Core32% of Rev

Commission-free, mobile-first. Self-clearing via RHS. Options $1,123M (25%), Equities $302M (7%). PFOF-based revenue. 24-hour trading. Citadel Securities = 13% of total revenue.

2. Cryptocurrency Trading
Core20% of Rev

58 cryptos (US) / 74 (EU). Staking, perpetual futures (Bitstamp), self-custody wallet. FY2025: $901M. Highly cyclical — ranged $160M (Q2) to $358M (Q4'24). Bitstamp adds institutional capability.

3. Prediction Markets & Futures
Growth / Emerging

"Fastest-growing business in our history" — $300M+ annualized run rate in first year. Rothera JV (HOOD 45%/Susquehanna 45%/MIAX 10%) captures full $0.02/contract economics (vs current $0.01). Active state litigation from gaming regulators.

4. Margin Lending & Securities Lending
Core17% of Rev

Margin interest $573M (13%) + securities lending $190M (4%). Margin book: $16.8B (+113% YoY). Near-zero incremental cost. Credit losses: $114M provision FY2025.

5. Cash Sweep & Interest Income
Core / Mature16% of Rev

FDIC-insured sweep ($2.25M coverage), segregated cash, corporate cash. Combined $715M. Rate-sensitive: 100bp = ~$304M impact (6.8% of rev).

6. Robinhood Gold (Subscription)
CoreMonetization Flywheel

Premium tier: higher APY, 3% IRA match, lower margin rates, Gold Card access. $179M FY2025 (4%). 4.18M subscribers (+58% YoY). ~$43/year per sub. Near-100% gross margin. Key flywheel — Gold subs generate higher ARPU.

7. Gold Card, Banking & Wealth Management
Growth / Emerging

No-fee credit card (600K holders, targeting >1M by YE 2026). Banking via BaaS (Coastal Bank). IRA with match. Strategies (0.25% fee). Robinhood Ventures Fund I (private markets). All gated behind Gold subscription.

8. TradePMR (RIA Custodial)
Growth / B2B

First B2B channel. $42.5B in RIA assets under administration. Complements retail with higher-AUM, lower-churn RIA clients. Not yet material to consolidated revenue.
