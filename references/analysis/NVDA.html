Thesis & Key Metric

annual / thesis change

Falsifiable Thesis Statement

NVIDIA is the dominant platform for AI infrastructure, with the CUDA ecosystem's 20-year switching costs, annual architecture cadence delivering order-of-magnitude perf/watt improvements, and expanding full-stack integration sustaining 70%+ market share through successive generation transitions. Data Center revenue will compound at 40%+ YoY through FY28 as AI buildout broadens from training to inference and expands across sovereign AI, enterprise, and physical AI markets.

Falsification triggers: DC Revenue YoY <25% for 2Q | Non-GAAP GM <66% for 2Q | Third-party share <65% + top-5 hyperscaler >30% GPU procurement cut

3-Sentence Bear Case

Hyperscaler custom silicon (Google TPU, Trainium, Broadcom ASICs) erodes demand as inference parity reduces NVIDIA share, while margin compresses to mid-60s from rack-scale complexity and export controls permanently close the $50B China market. Capex cycle pressure intensifies as ASIC competition forces pricing concessions and circular financing ($17.5B investments flowing back as chip purchases) masks organic demand sustainability.
