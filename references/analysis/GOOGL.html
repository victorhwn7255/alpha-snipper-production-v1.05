S2Thesis & Key Metricannual / thesis change
Falsifiable Thesis Statement

Alphabet's dominant search franchise and seven products with 2B+ monthly users provide an unassailable distribution moat for AI monetization, while Google Cloud's acceleration to 48% YoY (backed by $240B backlog) validates full-stack AI capabilities — custom silicon (TPU Ironwood) to frontier models (Gemini) to distribution — sustaining 15%+ total revenue growth and 30%+ OI margins through 2027, with Cloud growing to $90B+ that justifies the $175-185B CapEx cycle.

Falsification triggers: Cloud YoY <25% for 2Q | Search <5% + TAC rising >200bps | CapEx >$150B + Cloud <30% + FCF margin <10% | Adj OI margin <28% for 3Q
3-Sentence Bear Case

AI search competition erodes ~89% share and margin structure; DOJ restricts distribution defaults, compressing Search below 10% and forcing TAC increases. $175-185B CapEx destroys FCF (~$73B→~$24B) without proportional Cloud acceleration as overcapacity drives pricing pressure and open-source models commoditize inference. Cloud margins compress from 30% back toward breakeven while Other Bets losses (Waymo -$3.6B Q4 OI) accumulate without a clear path to profitability.
