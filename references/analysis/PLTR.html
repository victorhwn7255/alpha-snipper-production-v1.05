CoreScale

Central enterprise operating system — integrates siloed data via Ontology. Expansion: utility $7M → $31M ACV (4.4×); energy $4M → $20M (5×). Near-100% gross margin. Rev/employee $1.01M.

2. Gotham (Defense & Intelligence Platform)
CoreMature / Scale

Integrates battlefield and intelligence data across domains. Maven (AI C2 system) rolled out to NATO and all combatant commands. Navy contract: up to $448M. Government segment margin 65.6%.

3. AIP (Artificial Intelligence Platform)
Growth / CoreThesis-Critical

Bridges LLMs and enterprise operations via Ontology. Bootcamps (2-5 day workshops) convert to production: healthcare co → $96M deal in <6 months; engineering services → $80M deal. Primary driver of US commercial acceleration (64% → 137% YoY over 5Q). ~75% bootcamp conversion rate, >1,300 completed.

4. Apollo (Continuous Delivery Platform)
Infrastructure

Cloud-agnostic deployment (cloud, on-prem, classified, edge, air-gapped). Enables PLTR's deployment flexibility. Now offered standalone for customers' own software.

5. Warp Speed (Manufacturing OS for Defense Industrial Base)
Emerging

Ship OS: planning 160hrs → 10min; root cause analysis <20% → 99% in <1 week. American Tech Fellowship upskilling submarine industrial base users. Creates third-shift capacity.

6. Emerging (Hivemind, AIFD, OSDK, MAGE)
Optionality

Hivemind: AI agent swarms. AIFD: SAP ERP migrations in 2 weeks vs years. OSDK: 1B+ API requests/week from customer-built apps. MAGE: live-fire UAV coordination with autonomous battlefield response.
