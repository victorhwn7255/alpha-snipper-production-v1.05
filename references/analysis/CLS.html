Thesis & Key Metric

annual / thesis change

Falsifiable Thesis Statement

Celestica's transformation from traditional EMS contract manufacturer to AI infrastructure design partner — via its HPS networking switch portfolio (41% cumulative market share in 200G+ custom Ethernet, 55% in custom solutions) and custom ASIC compute partnerships (Google TPU, digital native customer) — creates a structurally higher-margin business with design-driven switching costs that will sustain 30%+ CCS revenue growth and 50-100bps annual non-GAAP operating margin expansion through FY2027.

Falsification trigger: CCS Communications YoY growth <30% for 2 consecutive quarters with simultaneous margin decline, OR loss of "preferred partner" status with any top-3 hyperscaler.
Date written: March 12, 2026  ·  Last reviewed: March 12, 2026

3-Sentence Bear Case

Google (~32% of FY2025 revenue) diversifies TPU manufacturing to competitors like Inventec or Foxconn, as previewed by the January 2026 Digitimes report that caused a ~6% stock decline. The $1B capex plan proves ill-timed if hyperscaler AI capex decelerates post-2027, stranding capacity and compressing ROIC from 43% toward the mid-20s. Simultaneously, 1.6T switch margins erode as Wiwynn, Accton, or Taiwanese ODMs close the manufacturing complexity gap, reducing pricing power and collapsing the 30× forward P/E premium.

Core / Growth~41% of Rev

Custom Ethernet switches for AI data centers. 41% cumulative market share in 200G+ custom Ethernet, 55% in custom solutions. First operational 1.6T switch prototype (Tomahawk 6). 10 active 1.6T programs, design wins with 3 hyperscalers. 1,100+ design engineers. Liquid cooling integration (DS6001).

2. AI/ML Custom Compute (Google TPU)
GrowthScale / Emerging

Custom ASIC-based compute systems — Google TPU is anchor program. Rack-scale integration, advanced liquid cooling, testing, orchestration. Q4 2025 Enterprise +33% YoY with acceleration expected FY2026 (100+teens% Q1 growth guided). "Preferred" but not sole source.

3. Digital Native Rack-Scale AI System
Emerging

Full rack-scale custom AI system for unnamed digital native customer (analyst/media speculation links to OpenAI; unconfirmed by CLS). "At least a few billion dollars in the first year" (2027). Samples H2 2026. CLS's highest-complexity engagement — proof point for full platform solutions capability.

4. Traditional CCS EMS
Core / Mature

Standard EMS for IT infrastructure — optical (Ciena), storage, data center racks. Lower margin than HPS. Being de-emphasized as HPS mix grows.

5. ATS Segment (A&D, Capital Equipment, Industrial, HealthTech)
Cash Flow Stabilizer26% of Rev

Diversified industrial markets. $3.2B FY2025. Segment margin 5.3% (improved from 4.6%). Exiting low-margin A&D programs, adding engineering-led HealthTech (>$100M growth expected 2026) and data center power infrastructure.
